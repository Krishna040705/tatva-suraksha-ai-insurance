# Suraksha 2-Minute Demo Script

## Opening

"Suraksha is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform for gig workers. Instead of waiting for manual claims, the system monitors external disruptions and triggers income-loss payouts automatically."

## Step 1: Registration

- Open the onboarding form.
- Register a worker with city, zone type, weekly income, and UPI ID.
- Mention that the plan is aligned to weekly cash flow, not monthly insurance cycles.

## Step 2: Dynamic Premium

- Open the premium section.
- Show the weekly premium recommendation.
- Highlight safe-zone discount support and explainable factor notes.

## Step 3: Policy Activation

- Activate the weekly protection plan.
- Mention coverage amount and coverage hours.

## Step 4: Automated Trigger Simulation

- Run `Monsoon Surge`.
- Explain that Suraksha watches rainfall, flooding, heat, AQI, and curfew feeds.
- Show the disruption cards crossing their thresholds.

## Step 5: Zero-Touch Claims

- Move to the claims rail.
- Show the automated claim generated without manual form filing.
- Highlight payout reference, fraud score, and paid status.

## Step 6: Fairness Layer

- Explain that suspicious cases are flagged instead of auto-rejected.
- If available, show the manual release action for a flagged claim.

## Closing

"Suraksha protects gig workers from income loss using dynamic pricing, automated triggers, and instant payout logic built for real weekly earning behavior."
