Suraksha

ML-assisted weekly income protection for gig workers

Worker-first product

Designed around weekly cash flow, fast onboarding, and direct wage replacement.

Judging focus

Fraud detection, instant payouts, intelligent dashboards, viability, and social impact.

Problem

Disruption hurts gig workers immediately, but insurance still responds slowly.

  • Workers earn weekly and lose wages the same day rain, heat, or restrictions hit.
  • Traditional claim journeys are too document-heavy for small but urgent losses.
  • Insurers need fraud controls, which often creates friction for honest workers.
Persona

Aarav Singh, delivery rider, Mumbai

Reality

Low savings buffer, highly weather-sensitive shifts, and no tolerance for payout delays.

Need

Weekly coverage that replaces lost wages fast without creating more paperwork.

Solution

Parametric protection with fraud intelligence and instant payout simulation.

Worker side

Register, get a weekly premium, activate policy, trigger claim, receive instant simulated payout.

Insurer side

Watch loss ratios, claim forecast, city concentration, and worker trust signals in one dashboard.

AI And Fraud Architecture

Explainable triggers plus honest ML-assisted fraud review.

Rule-based parametric core

Rain, flooding, heat, AQI, and curfew thresholds trigger coverage eligibility.

ML-assisted fraud layer

Centroid anomaly scoring checks GPS drift, device integrity, suspicious clusters, and fake weather declarations against historical bands.

Payout Rails

Approved claims simulate instant wage replacement.

  • UPI Simulator
  • Razorpay Test Mode
  • Stripe Sandbox

Each claim records payout rail, transfer ID, settlement time, and fraud evidence.

Weekly Pricing

Pricing aligned to gig-worker cash flow.

  • Weekly premiums feel small, understandable, and consistent with how workers earn.
  • Risk updates can happen quickly as zone exposure changes.
  • Transparent trigger thresholds build trust because the worker sees why they are paying.
Business Viability

The platform tracks whether the portfolio can scale sustainably.

Metrics tracked

Weekly premium pool, settled payouts, live loss ratio, predictive loss ratio, and break-even worker count.

Commercial thesis

Start in dense delivery corridors, pool low-risk and moderate-risk workers, and use digital payouts to reduce ops cost.

Social Impact

Fast protection matters most for workers with the least buffer.

  • Prevents small disruptions from turning into debt cycles.
  • Reduces proof burden for honest workers.
  • Improves confidence with transparent approval and payout visibility.
